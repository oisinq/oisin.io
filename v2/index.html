About me

Hiya! I'm Oisín Quinn. I’m a front-end software engineer living out in the countryside of Cavan, Ireland. I currently work remotely for HubSpot, where I build things™ in React and TypeScript.

I've tried my hand and most kinds of software development, but the web is where it all started. As a kid, I was obsessed with Club Penguin and spent my spare time creating my own Club Penguin fan blogs. Not to sound too cliché but those days really opened my eyes to the unlimited posibilities of what can be done on the web.

Since then, I've grown a bit taller, added some facial hair and completed a computer science degree. I've finally got the skills to turn my ideas into something that I can actually use, which is a really great feeling!
